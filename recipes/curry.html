Japanese Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 lb beef stew chunks
  • 5 potatoes
  • 3 carrots
  • 1/3 cup green peas
  • 1 pack curry roux
  • 5 cups of water

Steps:

  • Sautee onions
  • Brown beef stew
  • Add water and boil
  • Cut potatoes into bigger chunks and add into pot
  • Cut carrots into smaller pcs and add into pot
  • Add green peas
  • Once the potaoes and carrot are soft turn the heat off and add the roux
