Sinigang

Filipino soup dish that uses tamarind as a broth.

Best served with rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb preferred protein(pref fish, shrimp or pork
  • 1/4 cup of tamarind paste or a packet of readymade mix
  • 10 cups of water
  • 1/2" Ginger if seafood is used
  • 6 batches Bok choy
  • 1 medium onion
  • Daikon
  • 5 Taro root
    •

    Steps:

    1. Sautee onion till soft
    2. If using pork as protein, brown.
    3. Add water and satchel/paste and stir.
    4. Add ginger if seafood is being used
    5. Add daikon and bring to boil.
    6. Once daikon is a little softer add taro
    7. Once taro is soft add protein if seafood is used
    8. Add Bok Choy and turn heat off
