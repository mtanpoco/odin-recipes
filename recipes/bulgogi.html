Bulgogi

Korean BBQ marinaded in sesame oil with sugar and soy sauce.

Best served with rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb thinly sliced Ribeye or Sirloin steak
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Sprinkle of pickled ginger and sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions

Steps:

  1. In a gallon ziplock bag. Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, ginger, and sesame seeds.
  2. Marinade for atleast an hour
  3. In a lightly oiled pan place the steak evenly throughout the pan. Do not stack
  4. When finished. garnish with green onions.
